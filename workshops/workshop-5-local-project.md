# Workshop 5: Local Project

## Build a Real Website for a Local Shop or Business

**Duration:** 2 hours  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**What you'll learn:** Combine all skills to create a complete website project

---

## Project Overview

In this final workshop, you'll create a complete website for a real local business in Koh Phangan. You'll use everything you've learned: HTML, CSS, GitHub Pages, and AI tools.

---

## Step 1: Choose Your Business

**Option 1: Choose an existing business**
- Ask a local caf√©, shop, or service if they want a website
- Get their information (menu, hours, location, photos)
- Build it for them as a real project

**Option 2: Create a business idea**
- Imagine a business you'd like to start
- Create a website for it
- Use it as your portfolio piece

**Option 3: Partner with a classmate**
- Work together on one business
- Practice collaboration

---

## Step 2: Plan Your Website

**Before coding, plan these pages:**

1. **Homepage** - Welcome message, brief intro
2. **About** - Story of the business
3. **Menu/Services** - What they offer
4. **Location/Hours** - Where and when
5. **Contact** - How to reach them

**Write down:**
- Colors that match the business
- Key information to include
- Photos needed (you can use placeholder images)

---

## Step 3: Gather Information

**What you need:**

- Business name
- Description of what they do
- Menu items or services (with prices if possible)
- Opening hours
- Location/address
- Contact information (email, phone, social media)
- Photos (optional - can use free stock photos)

**Use AI to help:**
- Ask ChatGPT to write descriptions
- Ask for menu item descriptions
- Translate content to Thai/Burmese if needed

---

## Step 4: Create the HTML Structure

**Create `index.html`:**

```html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<head>
    <meta charset="UTF-8">
    <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
    <title>[Business Name] - Koh Phangan</title>
    <link rel="stylesheet" href="style.css">
</head>
<body>
    <header>
        <nav>
            <h1>[Business Name]</h1>
            <ul>
                <li><a href="#home">Home</a></li>
                <li><a href="#about">About</a></li>
                <li><a href="#menu">Menu</a></li>
                <li><a href="#contact">Contact</a></li>
            </ul>
        </nav>
    </header>
    
    <main>
        <section id="home">
            <h2>Welcome</h2>
            <p>[Welcome message]</p>
        </section>
        
        <section id="about">
            <h2>About Us</h2>
            <p>[Business story]</p>
        </section>
        
        <section id="menu">
            <h2>Our Menu</h2>
            <!-- Add menu items -->
        </section>
        
        <section id="contact">
            <h2>Visit Us</h2>
            <p>Hours: [Opening hours]</p>
            <p>Location: [Address]</p>
            <p>Contact: [Email/Phone]</p>
        </section>
    </main>
    
    <footer>
        <p>&copy; 2024 [Business Name]</p>
    </footer>
</body>
</html>
```

**Fill in the placeholders with real information!**

---

## Step 5: Style with CSS

**Create `style.css` with:**

- Colors that match the business
- Good spacing and layout
- Mobile-friendly design
- Professional look

**Copy from Workshop 2 or create your own style!**

---

## Step 6: Add Content

**Use AI to help write:**
- Ask ChatGPT for menu descriptions
- Get ideas for "About Us" section
- Write social media posts

**Make it personal:**
- Add real information about the business
- Include local touches (mention Koh Phangan)
- Use Thai/Burmese translations if helpful

---

## Step 7: Make It Look Professional

**Add these elements:**

1. **Navigation bar** - Easy to move between pages
2. **Hero section** - Big welcome at the top
3. **Good spacing** - Not too crowded
4. **Readable fonts** - Clear and easy to read
5. **Consistent colors** - Match throughout

**Check:**
- Does it look good on mobile?
- Is the text easy to read?
- Are links working?
- Is information easy to find?

---

## Step 8: Test Your Website

**Test checklist:**

- [ ] All links work
- [ ] Looks good on phone
- [ ] Looks good on computer
- [ ] Information is correct
- [ ] No spelling mistakes
- [ ] Colors look good
- [ ] Text is readable

**Ask a friend to review:**
- Does it make sense?
- Is it easy to use?
- What would they change?

## Project Tips

**Keep it simple:**
- Don't try to do too much
- Focus on making it work well
- Better simple and working than complex and broken

**Get feedback:**
- Show friends and family
- Ask the business owner (if real project)
- Listen to suggestions

**Iterate:**
- Your first version doesn't have to be perfect
- Keep improving it
- Add features later

---

## What Makes a Good Website?

**Good website has:**
- Clear purpose (what is this business?)
- Easy navigation (can I find what I need?)
- Good information (hours, location, contact)
- Nice design (looks professional)
- Works on mobile (most people use phones)
